Character List

Edward Wong Hau Pepelu Tivrusky IV known as Radical Edward, was a child prodigy skilled in hacking originally from Earth.

Faye Valentine is a wanted bounty head and a member of the bounty hunting crew aboard the Bebop.

Spike Spiegel is a former member of the criminal Red Dragon Syndicate, who left by faking his death after falling in love with a woman named Julia.

Jet Black is an officer of the ISSP and later bounty hunter.
